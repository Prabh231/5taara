About 5 Taara Punjabi Restaurant

Authentic Punjabi flavours, traditional recipes, and a passion for serving delicious food.

Our Story

Its kind of funny story. We have a friends reunion after 2 years. There we planed about doing a business and planned everything but forgot in morning. And decided a name from popular punjabi song "5 taara" by diljit. We wont admit but it was dream to crate a friendly and homely vibe place After 2 years, we finally completed our dream which started as a joke. 5 Taara Punjabi Restaurant was created to bring the true taste of Punjab to food lovers. Our journey started with a love for traditional Punjabi cooking and family recipes passed down through generations.

Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients, authentic spices, and the same warmth found in Punjabi homes.

Punjabi Heritage

Punjabi cuisine represents culture, celebration, and togetherness. From rich curries to famous street foods, every meal tells a story.

Our restaurant celebrates Punjab's traditions by combining classic cooking techniques with a modern dining experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality Punjabi food while creating a welcoming environment where families and friends can enjoy memorable dining experiences.

Why Customers Love Us

  • Authentic Punjabi recipes
  • Fresh and quality ingredients
  • Friendly family atmosphere
  • Traditional flavours with modern service
