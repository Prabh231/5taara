Gol Gappa

Gol Gappa, also known as Pani Puri, is a popular Indian street food made with crispy puris filled with potatoes, chickpeas, and refreshing spicy and tangy flavoured water.

Product Details

Ingredients

  • Crispy puris
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Mint water
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Punjabi spices

Serving Size

One serving includes eight freshly prepared Gol Gappas with spicy mint water and sweet chutney.
